We’re a small team trying to build something from the ground up, and we’re 100 % volunteer‑run. Below are the roles we urgently need—hover for details and time commitments.

Immigration Attorney / BIA‑Accredited Rep

Turns evidence into legal shields.

  • File bond motions, stays, I‑246s, motions to reopen & suppress
  • Advise rapid‑response team on legality during raids
  • Coordinate with congressional offices for casework inquiries
  • Coordinate with other leadership team members
  • Offer assistance to the attorneys of ICE detainees

Media Lead

Turns facts into public pressure.

  • Draft press releases, op‑eds, & rapid rebuttals
  • Curate & publish verified raid footage
  • Train spokespeople, prep families for interviews
  • Maintain live social threads during actions
  • Publish social media content
  • Coordinate with other leadership team members

Software Lead / Tech Maintainer

Keeps the digital backbone humming.

  • Own the website
  • Build / maintain custom tools (e.g., secure evidence upload, bots for the social media team)
  • Coordinate with other leadership team members

Protest & Event Lead

Puts our message in the streets.

  • Plan vigils, press conferences, court‑support rallies
  • Secure permits, liaise with marshals & legal observers
  • Coordinate art, signage, chants, accessible logistics
  • Coordinate with other leadership team members

Videographers / Field Responders

Eyes and evidence on the ground.

  • Deploy on hotline alerts, film from public space, collect witness info
  • Upload raw footage to the secure evidence upload tool within the hour
  • Request ICE ID themselves and show warrants/badges
  • Think fast to make the surrounding public aware of ICE's presence(safety).

Recruiters/Intelligence

Keep the pipeline of volunteers full.

  • Pass out recruitment information
  • Spread important information to the community as it becomes available
  • Gather information and recruit members at community events, faith centers, campuses
